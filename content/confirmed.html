Congratulations! Your new account has been setup and is now ready to use. Below is a brief overview of what you can do as a registered user of haiku-os.org.

Personal profile

Your account has a personal profile that you can configure to make the most out of haiku-os.org. Here are the settings that you can configure in your personal profile:

  • Account information: you can change your username, the email address associated with your account, and your password.
  • Comments settings: you can setup a signature that will be inserted automatically at the foot of your comments.
  • Subscription settings: check the "Autosubscribe" box if you you want to be automatically notified by email of any comments posted to content that you created or as a reply to any of your comments.
  • Contact settings: check the "Personal contact form" box if you want other users to be able to contact you using a personal contact form.
  • Locale settings: Use it to set the time zone you are located in.

Participating

With your new account, you can now:

  • Post comments: you are now able to post comments to news posts, documents and on the forums.
  • Subscribe to content: Some news and documents have a "Subscribe post" link that enables an automatic email notification every time the post is changed, or a comment is posted to it. This is great way to follow up posts of special interest to you.
  • Submit content: you can submit news posts or documents for publication on the Haiku website. See the Contributing Content page for details.
Spam accounts: We have implemented various spam control measures in our website. Accounts used to post spam comments or spam content on any of our pages or on user profiles will be deleted immediately.

Enjoy the Haiku website!
